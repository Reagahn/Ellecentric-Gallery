Predator (2022-09-09)

This art work relates to one of the four laws of ecology: "Nature knows best." A predator hunting its prey is only natural. It may not be pleasing to the human eye, but that's how nature works, and it has its own reason for happening.

Blaze of Heart (2023-01-26)

This artwork's theme is "Blaze of Heart". It interprets that when a person tries to enter your heart and you let them inside, it thaws the frozen heart and turns it into a molten representation of emotions.

Abstract Cubism (2022-03-02)

This piece is a combination of abstract and cubism art. The candle represents a person slowly dissolving under humanity's gaze regarding their identity and being forced to fit social norms. It can also be perceived by explanations people want to perceive in any way.

Monochrome Sea Waves (2023-04-29)

This art work is only composed of one color, thus being monochromatic and having different shades. Which results in a sky above the sea waves.
